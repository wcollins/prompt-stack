# The Cloud Gambit - From SOAR to Agents: Why Practical Automation Has to Survive Contact with Real Infrastructure

### Cold Open

Everyone's building AI agents right now. Most of them work great in the demo. Then you point them at a real network, a real SIEM, a real ITSM - and they fall apart. Today I'm talking to someone who's been connecting real systems to real automation for 25 years, and who has some very direct opinions about why the gap between "it works in the demo" and "I'd run this in production" is bigger than most people admit. Let's dig in.
